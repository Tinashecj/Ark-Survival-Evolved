Aberration

Survivors will spawn on a malfunctioning ARK in an underground biome. The atmosphere has leaked away, making way for intense radiation and a harsh environment. This has led to unique genetic mutations as creature and fauna have had to adapt to survive. Among them are the camouflaging Rock Drake who can climb up walls and glide through the air. Light the way with the help of the friendly Lantern Pug. Grab and toss multiple creatures with the massive Cave Crustacean. Although it will take a skilled player to tame one, the Nameless Queen would be worth the effort as her ability impregnates foes with a horrible reproductive chest-burster.

Aberration includes new tools and engrams. Create zip lines and gliding wingsuits for travel, hazmat suits, railguns and explosive rounds for defense and protection, explore caves using cave-climbing picks to get around. Use fishing baskets and gas collectors to gather new resources.

Players will find themselves far from alone in Aberration. Countless "Nameless" creatures roam about and they are the among the greatest challenges players will face. They will require constant vigilance, and have a hierarchy system that includes a powerful master in the deepest depths of Aberration. Players will need to find ways to defend against their continuous attacks.
